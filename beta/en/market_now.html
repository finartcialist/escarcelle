festive bell

Linked souvenir: This little bell reminds of all the kind little family things that happens during holidays, big and small. It comes from chocolat candies and we often re-use them to decorate a variety of gifts. I collec them, here is one!

Identification number: 0a

Klein bottle

Linked souvenir: I was a mathematics student, and topology was one of my favorite topics. A Klein bottle is not orientable, meaning we cannot say that one side of its surface is outside while the other is inside in a coherent way. I bought this bottle out of curiosity, and it has proven to be a great converstaion started. I would be happy to see it make its own path in another home.

Identification number : 0b

playing card

Linked memory: A few years ago, I was working on a board game whose theme was the feelings associated with doing mathematics research. This is one of the cards of one of the first prototypes of this game.

Identification number : 0c
