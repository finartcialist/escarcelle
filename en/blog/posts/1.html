trading - objects ideas

No need to say goodbye to your most precious objects to take a part in Escarcelle!

As long as the object brings back memories for you, you can submit the object for exchange.

There is room for all kinds of memories - and all sorts of objects.

A spot is kept in the market for trading postcards: you can send a postcard and receive one in exchange.

As a way to mark the launch of the Escarcelle beta version, the ten (10) first exchange that will happen will receive a coupon for up to 12$CAD of the total of shipping costs associated with sending the object chosen from the market.
