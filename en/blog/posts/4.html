creating a postcard

"I would love to participate, but I don't have any postcard available for exchange! What can I do?"

For the beta version, the focus is less about the object itself than about the memories.

This is why there are many ways to participate by creating a postcard.

You may buy a new postcard, writing your souvenir - real or not, or wished for - on it.

You may buy or re-use a rectangular cardboard piece in order to decorate it. Don't forget to add your souvenir!

You may use a picture in order to create a postcard.

I hope that this inspires you!

I will answer your questions by email at escarcelle@finartcialist.com - I will be happy to discuss the project with you.
