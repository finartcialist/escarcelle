[Nov 22nd, 2021]

The first postcards are here! What can we currently find at Escarcelle? The answer is at current state state of the market - beta version.

Note that I interpreted figuratively the ideas associated with feeling and with souvenir... One of the postcard is about the past; the other, about an (uncertain_ future; the last one is wondering about made-up memories.

You have some leverage in how you interpret what is a feeling, what is a souvenir. It's a poetic exercise, in a way.

You can also stick to the facts when describing a memory - or, at least, try to.

Feel welcome to ask me questions by email at escarcelle@finartcialist.com - I will be happy to discuss the project with you.
